# MANTHANA — Website copy & product overview

Use this document as source material for a marketing site, landing pages, or investor briefs. Product facts are aligned with the **Manthana** Next.js app (`Cosmic Medical Intelligence`).

---

## One-line positioning

**MANTHANA** is a medical intelligence platform that **churns five oceans of medicine**—Ayurveda, Allopathy, Homeopathy, Siddha, and Unani—and surfaces **verified, citation-aware answers** alongside **AI-assisted imaging workflows** and **deep research** tools.

---

## The idea (brand story)

The name evokes **Samudra Manthan**: the cosmic churning of the ocean in which wisdom and hazard rise together. Manthana treats modern medicine the same way—not as a single textbook, but as **many parallel traditions and evidence bases** that must be **processed, compared, and refined** so what rises to the top is **clarity, not noise**.

That is the **“Amrita”** metaphor in-product: **pure, living knowledge** drawn from diverse sources—not a monoculture of one school of thought.

## What makes MANTHANA different

| Differentiator | What it means in practice |
| ---------------- | ------------------------- |
| **Five medical systems in one surface** | Not “Ayurveda chat” or “PubMed chat” alone—you can work in **Allopathy**, **Ayurveda**, **Homeopathy**, **Siddha**, or **Unani**, or invoke **M5** to query **all five at once**. |
| **M5 — “All 5”** | A first-class mode for **cross-tradition synthesis** when the question benefits from multiple lenses (e.g., drug–herb context, classical vs. RCT framing). |
| **Oracle + Labs, not two unrelated apps** | **Manthana Labs** (imaging / analysis) can **hand off structured context to Oracle** so conversation continues with **case-aware grounding** (session-based handoff; educational / decision-support framing). |

| **Med Deep Research** | Dedicated **research workspace** with domains, source filters, citation styles (e.g., Vancouver, ICMR), and depth levels—suited to **evidence review**, not quick chat alone. |
| **Manthana Labs — scale of modalities** | Imaging pipeline supports **broad modality coverage** including **auto-detection** across many study types, **multi-modality unified reporting**, **AI orchestration** across modalities, and **premium / enterprise-style** paths (e.g., advanced CT workflows, PACS-oriented features on higher tiers). |

## Product pillars (what the platform includes)

### 1. Oracle — conversational medical intelligence

- Natural-language **Q&A** with streaming responses and **source-aware** behavior where applicable.
- **Domain selection**: work inside one tradition or switch to **M5** for a **five-system** response pattern.
- **Modes** include standard chat, **Manthana Web** (literature search results), and **Med Deep Research** (structured research flows)—so one shell supports **answer**, **search**, and **research**.

### 2. M5 — query all five systems simultaneously

- Purpose-built for questions where **single-tradition answers are insufficient**—integration, comparison, or exploratory learning across **Ayurveda · Allopathy · Homeopathy · Siddha · Unani**.

### 3. Manthana Web — search the evidence bases

- Designed for **clinical and academic search**: trials, guidelines, and indexed literature—not a generic search engine skin.
- Placeholder UX examples in-product: PubMed, Cochrane, WHO, ICMR, India-relevant trials, formulation and guideline lookup.

### 4. Med Deep Research — structured research sessions

- **Threads**, **domains**, and **citation formatting** for longer-form synthesis.
- Supports **serious review workflows** (depth and source discipline) rather than only short chat turns.

### 5. Manthana Labs — AI-assisted imaging & analysis (`/analyse`)

- **Viewer + analysis** experience with findings panels, **AI report** surfaces, **orchestration progress**, and **unified multi-modality** summaries where applicable.
- **Auto-detect** modality routing and **large modality catalog** (including orchestration-first paths and premium CT-related workflows).
- **Patient context** forms (including specialty flows such as ECG-oriented context where implemented).
- **Consent** and **gateway** integration patterns for responsible access.
- **History** and **worklist / PACS-oriented** components on supported tiers.
- **Labs → Oracle handoff**: after analysis, users can continue in Oracle with **structured report markdown** carried into chat context (educational / decision-support disclaimers apply).

## Who it is for

- **Clinicians and trainees** who need **fast triangulation** across traditions and evidence—with **citations** and **mode controls**.
- **Researchers and students** who need **literature search** and **deep research** outputs with **disciplined sourcing**.
- **Imaging-adjacent workflows** where **AI-assisted draft findings** and **unified reporting** accelerate review—always subject to **human verification** and local policy.

---

## Plans (high level)

The product is offered in tiers (see in-app **Plans** for live pricing and limits), roughly:

- **Free**: trial access to **Manthana Labs** (limited lifetime trial scans for signed-in users), **Oracle** with **M5** on basic models and **fair-use** rate limits.
- **Pro / Pro Plus**: expanded **Labs** monthly allowances, **full Oracle** capabilities, **multi-model** analysis, and higher tiers add **PACS-oriented workflows**, **priority processing**, and **premium Oracle models** (per plan).
- **Premium / Custom**: **enterprise-style** volumes, **3D volumetric** and **full DICOM video** where offered, and **sales-led** contracts.

> **Note:** Limits, modalities, and prices change—**the subscription UI is the source of truth.**

## Trust, safety, and compliance framing

- Manthana Labs outputs are framed as **educational and decision-support** material; they are **not a substitute** for a **certified radiology report** or **independent clinical judgment**.
- Users should follow **local regulations**, **institutional policies**, and **professional standards** for diagnosis and treatment.
- The application may ship with **`noindex`** metadata in certain builds—adjust SEO tags when you publish a **public** marketing domain.
